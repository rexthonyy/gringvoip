A new kind of social network

Social networks are great. They are a place for connecting with like-minded individuals and sharing knowledge.

For me, they've enabled building an audience of people interested in my journey as a maker and helped me meet amazing people. Others have met their business partners or given people jobs.

But, in the last few years, they've become a source of disinformation, anonymous harassment, privacy concerns, and anxiety, while the platforms focused only on growing their engagement and revenue.

It's time for a new platform, one that respects your privacy, that doesn't try to get you addicted, and that protects you from hate and harassment. A platform made for users, not for advertisers.

And, while we're at it, let's also include everything we've been asking those other platforms for ages.

Meet GringVoip.

GringVoip is a social network which leverages on physical human interaction using voice and video as the primary medium of communication.

GringVoip is built for people, starting with its revenue model. Instead of making money off ads, GringVoip is user-funded, ensuring the focus is always on providing the best experience for our users.

With no ads, there's also no need for any tracking or selling of your data. We're the first privacy-first social network.

Control is a big element of GringVoip. Aside from complete control over your data, you also get to choose who you interact with, from everyone to mutual followers or even a small hand-picked selection.

While we believe this should help prevent harassment, we also require each user to verify their identity before posting to GringVoip, deterring abuse, and making our network more about people and less about profiles.

GringVoip is also built to be open, extensible and developer friendly, embracing open standards with powerful bot control and an open API to empower developers to interface seamlessly with the platform.

These are just some of the things we've planned for GringVoip. If you want to learn more about what we're building, have a voice in the development on the platform, and be the first one to get access when we open the gates, leave your email below.

Frequently asked questions

What is your policy going to be regarding usernames?
When we start inviting those in the early-access list, they'll be able to reserve their usernames on a first come first served basis. Your username must be two characters minimum and unique across GringVoip. Usernames will be redistributed on account deletion.
What platforms will GringVoip be available on?
While the GringVoip private beta is currently only running on web, we plan to roll out Android and iOS apps after the public launch.
